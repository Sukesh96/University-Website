Fresh Admission 2021-2022

  • New Admission will start from 20th February 2021.
  • Last Date for Admission (except Certificate and Semester-based Programmes) - 31st March, 2021
  • Note: In a particular admission cycle, facility for fee exemption available to SC/ST students can be claimed for one programme only. If an applicant submits more than one application claiming fee exemption, all the applications are liable to be rejected.

Instructions For Registration

  • If you are a first time applicant you are advised to click the available programme tab on the homepage of the Online Admission System and select the desired programme and carefully read the details of programme including eligibility criteria, fee details, duration, etc.
  • You are also requested to download the Common Prospectus and read carefully the Rules of the University (Section 06) as mentioned in the common prospectus. You may also read sections 1, 7, 8, 9, 12 and 13.
    1. Click on the button NEW REGISTRATION that appears in the applicant login area and fill the required registration details.
    2. Remember, while choosing your UserName it must be between 8 to 16 characters.
    3. While choosing your password it must be alphanumeric and between 8 to 16 characters long.
    4. After filling the mandatory information click the "SUBMIT" button.
    5. Your username will be instantly sent to you via e-mail and SMS.

Payment Options

  • Remember your Username and Password for subsequent login.
  • Fee can be paid by following methods:
    1. Credit Card (Master/Visa)
    2. Debit Card (Master/Visa)
    3. Net Banking/ UPI
